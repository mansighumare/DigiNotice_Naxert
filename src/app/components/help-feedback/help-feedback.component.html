1. What are legal paper notices for properties?

Legal paper notices of any property are the preliminary step of any property transaction. It mentions main details of the property such as owners name, area of property, location details. It also mentions partners of the property or hypothecation of property. Generally it is published to inform people about the transaction in process and they may approach for any objections concerned authority.

Instead of going through all newspapers for notices, diginotice compiles paper notices from newspapers of the city. They are displayed in diginotice in most simplified digital format. The additional option of search notices and asset managers also help to manage the property.

4. How does “SEARCH NOTICES” work?

The search notices is an important part of diginotice. It facilitates to allow you to search for past notices by filling up the required details. This shortlist the notices corresponding to the filled details.

5. What is ASSET MANAGER?

ASSET MANAGER works as your personal manager. It works for you even though you don’t check for paper notices daily. Just by filling details of property of which you are the owner or guardian the asset manager notifies you of any proceedings on the saved property.
